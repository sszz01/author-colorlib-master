About The Book

Ever wonder why some individuals effortlessly command attention and influence, while others struggle to be heard? The answer lies in mastering the art of communication. No one wants to be misunderstood. Yet, most people are unaware of how often their message is lost to verbal and non-verbal distractions and bad habits. Credibility, and ultimately influence, is easily lost with communication missteps.

In this book, you will uncover the secrets to achieving clear and impactful communication, gaining recognition for your achievements, and standing out as a credible and influential individual in any setting.

Know More About The Author

Roger S. Peterson

Roger S. Peterson is a Professional Certified Marketer (PCM™) of the American Marketing Association, one of only 196 persons in America to gain this status. He has over 30 years of experience in sales, product management, advertising, and high-tech marketing communications (marcom) management. Unlike most marketers, Peterson had two successful sales careers and is a widely published business writer. His career includes roles as a marketing communications manager at two technology firms and as a writer for Intel’s IT department. He also managed divisions of Ogilvy & Mather and J. Walter Thompson.
